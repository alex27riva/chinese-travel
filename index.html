💡 Tip: In Chinese apps, 确认 (quèrèn) and 取消 (qǔxiāo) are often swapped compared to Western UI conventions — read carefully before tapping! 扫码 (sǎo mǎ) is everywhere: metro, restaurants, shops, bikes.
